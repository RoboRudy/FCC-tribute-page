Patrick Star

Is this the Krusty Krab? No this is Patrick.

Patrick lives under a rock in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom next door to Squidward Tentacles' moai

Origin of Patrick Star

  • Stephen Hillenburg first designed a prototypic starfish character while teaching marine science at the Ocean Institute in California.[5] He created the character for The Intertidal Zone, an educational picture book used to teach young students at the Institute, which would eventually lead to the creation of SpongeBob SquarePants.
  • This prototypic starfish character was much more realistic than Patrick: it had an eyespot on each arm and a large mouth in the center of its body. In 1987, Hillenburg left the institute to pursue a career in animation.
  • Hillenburg described Patrick as "probably the dumbest guy in town".
  • He first appeared in the series' pilot episode "Help Wanted" on May 1, 1999. Seen as a lazy, overweight and dimwitted pink starfish.
  • He is SpongeBob's best friend as well as one of his two neighbors.
  • He is voiced by Bill Fagerbakke and first appears in the pilot episode, "Help Wanted."
  • Both he and SpongeBob like to bother Squidward; though they do it unknowingly and have good intentions.
  • He is the son of Herb and Margie Star and the younger brother of Sam Star.
  • Patrick was kicked out of his parents' house at one point and never allowed to come back. He thus created his rock, with the only difference being that he added a satellite antenna to it.
  • One day, when his neighbor Squidward was gardening, a pineapple fell from a ship into the ocean, landing on Squidward's garden creating the pineapple house.
  • SpongeBob was looking for a house of his own; when all hope seemed lost, SpongeBob and the real estate agent saw the pineapple house's creation and Patty said she had forgotten about the house.
  • SpongeBob bought it. To this day, they have been neighbors.
